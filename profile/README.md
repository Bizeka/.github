## 🚀 About Bizeka

**Bizeka** is a boutique software development brand driven by a unique edge: it was founded by a former Finance Director and Digital Transformation Leader who transitioned into software engineering nearly a decade ago, after an extensive career in the corporate world. 

We don't just write code; we understand the underlying business mechanics. Combining over 20 years of deep corporate business expertise with nearly 10 years of professional software development in **Finance, CRM, MRP, and Custom Reporting**, Bizeka was born to build software that solves real-world corporate friction. 

Whether it's an enterprise-grade web application, a highly polished open-source UI library, or specialized modules, Bizeka delivers solutions engineered with both business acumen and technical excellence. Supported by **Fed Bilişim** for robust IT infrastructure and technical operations, we provide end-to-end digital lifecycle management.

## 🛠 What We Do

### 📦 Open Source & Commercial Libraries
We build niche, high-performance tools for modern web applications.
- **[FlipbookEngine](https://github.com/Bizeka/flipbook-engine)**: A premium, embeddable HTML flipbook viewer for PDF-backed catalogs with zero external dependencies.

### 🧩 Serenity Framework Ecosystem
Having followed the **Serenity Framework** since its early days, Bizeka specializes in creating modular, scalable, and enterprise-ready architectures.
- **Upcoming**: A suite of vertical and horizontal open-source/commercial Add-ons and Modules specifically designed for the Serenity ecosystem.
- **Consulting**: Deep architectural consulting and custom module development for enterprise Serenity projects.

### 🏢 Enterprise Solutions
- Custom CRM & MRP Systems
- Advanced Financial & Operational Reporting
- Corporate Launch & E-Commerce Platforms (.NET Core & WooCommerce)
